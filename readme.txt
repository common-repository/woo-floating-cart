== Description ==

**[Side Cart For Woocommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woo-floating-cart/)** Show side cart in website . when your add to cart product auto open side cart popup with animation in your [Woocommerce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WooCommerce) Website.

**[Demo Side Cart Woocommerce](http://oceanwebguru.com/oceanwebdemo/shop/)**

Customer can be show product which added cart in side cart there customer can be remove that product quickly in **Woocommerce Slide Out Cart**.
